Physician portal

Genospace

Many physicians see as many as 60 patients per day, which gives each patient roughly 20 minutes of a physician’s time. Physicians need an efficient, patient-centric system that will allows them spend less time doing administrative work and more time with patients.

Our team at Genospace was tasked with designing a portal for physicians to better suit client requirements and fulfill user needs.

  • Team
  • 3 designers
  • 1 user researcher
  • Responsibilities
  • UX designer
  • Design strategy
  • Usability testing
  • Prototyping

The team conducted baseline research with oncologists to better understand their habits and behavior. After analyzing the data, we found that our users also included nurses and administrators as they would assist by taking down patient details or ordering tests on behalf of the oncologist.

We also found that oncologists needed a way to compare a patient’s test history to find any patterns as their current system didn't allow for that digitally.

Approach

The team comprised of 3 designers and 1 user researcher. We kicked off a week-long design sprint with the goal of creating an interactive prototype to test with customers. We did mind maps, 8-ups and created user stories where we dot-votted to see which ideas stuck with us the most.

Some common themes

  • A dashboard for a physician to see an overview on all their patient’s data
  • Organizing reports into categories based on a physician’s workflow
  • A patient profile with their data, and a comprehensive history of all their previous tests
  • The ability to print reports to give to the patient
