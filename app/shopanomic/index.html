SHOPANOMIC

USER EXPERIENCE/INTERACTIVE/BRANDING

ALL YOUR SHOPPING NEEDS RIGHT IN THE PALM OF YOUR HAND KEEP UP TO DATE ON ALL THE LATEST TRENDS, STYLING AND SALES

As a self-initiated design project, I developed the icons and interface design for a shopping application that I created and named Shopanomic. I wanted to create a convenient shopping experience for the user so that everything they needed to decide on what to buy would be right in their phone (aside from physical fit, of course). There would be product details, shipping & costs, personalized feed and wishlists, as well as constantly updated information about sales, newly added pieces and current trends.
