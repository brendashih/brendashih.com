MY PANTONE PROJECT

PHOTOGRAPHY/COLOR/PERSONAL

As a visual person, I'm a firm believer in learning by seeing. Traveling alone (and on a budget) was probably one of the best things I ever did for myself because I've learned more about myself in a few months than the rest of my life. (For example, I found out I'm horrible at directions and can't read train schedules correctly.) I suffered and hated parts of my trips, but I saw some of the most beautiful sites in the world and met the most amazing people along the way.


This started out as just a photographic journal to myself, and from that, I ended up creating my own color exploration project. And for this 'Pantone' project, I broke down the main and most vibrant colors from the photos I took in order to bring my photos and therefore, my experiences more to life and more personal. This project is an exploration of color and photography that I felt really documented my own journey while I traveled through Europe and Asia.
