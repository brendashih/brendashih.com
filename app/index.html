My name is Brenda Shih.

I was born and raised in sunny Northern California, but I went to school at Parsons New School for Design in New York to study and work in fashion design.


As I dabbled more in technology, I found a real passion for graphic and UX/UI design, and in doing so I hope to learn as much as I can so I could solve technological problems as well as make things beautiful. You can view my work here and if you like, contact me directly for resumes, cover letters and references!
