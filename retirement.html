Retirement distributions

Fidelity Investments

RMD stands for required minimum distribution – when customers turn 70.5, they are required, by the IRS, to take money out of their retirement account by a certain date otherwise they are fined 50% of their required withdrawal.

Fidelity supports an average of 970,000 RMD customers per year with an average of 540,000 incoming support calls about this intent. In addition to that, we saw that 32% of customers who call were calling about reassurance and education. As evident from the number of support calls in 2017, Fidelity’s current legacy experience did not provide customers with the information they needed to feel confident they were withdrawing money successfully.

Approach

For MVP1, we decided to target 1st time RMD customers because they made up 24% of retirement support calls and are more likely to call than repeaters, having never gone through the process before.

To increase customer confidence and keep the user’s focus on their task at hand, we created a full page experience and kept the look and feel for each part of the transaction consistent. We also provided appropriate messaging for the customer when they had to sell investments to make cash available or increase their tax withholding on their distribution.

Results

Withdrawing a large chunk of money can be daunting for an older customer. They have to understand penalties, taxes and varying RMD amounts depending on the type of retirement account they have. We aimed to make the process less stressful for a customer and in the first month of our MVP1 launch, there was a 13.1% reduction in support calls.
